PANDA

The giant panda, also known as the panda bear or simply the panda, is a bear native to South Central China. It is characterised by its bold black-and-white coat and rotund body. The name "giant panda" is sometimes used to distinguish it from the red panda, a neighboring musteloid. Wikipedia

Mass: Female: 70 – 100 kg (Adult) Lifespan: 20 years (In the wild) Trophic level: Herbivorous Encyclopedia of Life Scientific name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca Height: 60 – 90 cm (Adult, At Shoulder) Gestation period: 95 – 160 days
